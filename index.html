United States National Soccer Team

Men's Team

The U.S. Men's National Team was first formed in 1885. In major competitions they have won the CONCACAF Gold Cup 6 times, have been the runner up in the Confederations Cup once, and placed third in the World Cup in 1930. Their odds of winnning the 2018 World Cup is 0%.

Records

  • Most Goals Scored in a Game: 8
  • Most Consecutive Wins: 12
  • Goals by a Single Player: 57

Coaches

The Men's National Team does not have a head coach at the moment. They have four assistant coaches, a goalkeeper coach, as well as other officials.

Women's Team

The U.S. Women's National Team first played in 1985. Since then they have won the CONCACAF Gold Cup 7 times, taken gold in the Olympics 4 times, and won the World Cup 3 times.

Records

  • Most Goals Scored in a Game: 14
  • Most Consecutive Wins: 18
  • Goals by a Single Player: 184

Coaches

Jill Ellisis the current Women's National Team head coach. She is assisted by Tony Gustavsson along with a goalkeeper coach and a strength and fitness coach.
